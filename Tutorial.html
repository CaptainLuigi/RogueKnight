Welcome to RogueKnight!

In this tutorial, you'll learn the basics:

  • In the circle above the knight, you can see how much energy you have.
  • Above the weapons is displayed how much Energy using this weapon costs. Hover over the weapon for more information.
  • click on the weapon to use it, sometimes you have to click the enemy you want to hit afterwards. You can also use the number pad for that.
  • Hover over the icons below the enemies to see what they will do in their turn.
  • When you are done attacking or you don't have energy left, click on the End Turn button or press the Space Bar.
  • Then the enemies perform their actions, your energy is refilled and it's your turn again.
  • Defeat all enemies to win the fight.
